Bringing FTC to Portugal

This webpage documents the process of introducing the First Tech Challenge (FTC) to Portugal and its impact on STEM education worldwide.

The Journey Begins

Initiating FTC in Portugal marked a significant step in expanding STEM education opportunities. It involved coordinating with FTC officials and engaging local schools and teams.

Empowering Portuguese Students

FTC in Portugal empowers students to explore robotics, engineering, and technology. It provides a platform for hands-on learning and problem-solving, fostering the growth of STEM skills.

Global Impact

By bringing FTC to Portugal, we contribute to the global pursuit of STEM education. It connects Portuguese students with peers worldwide and promotes collaboration on innovative solutions.
